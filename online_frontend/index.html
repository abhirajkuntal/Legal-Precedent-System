Architecture

Grotius is powered by a state-of-the-art Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline. At its core, the engine utilizes SentenceTransformers to convert vast amounts of legal text into high-dimensional vector embeddings, which are then organized within a highly optimized FAISS index.

About Us

Grotius was engineered to push the boundaries of machine learning and data science within the legal tech space. We recognized that traditional keyword-based legal research is fundamentally broken. By deploying a state-of-the-art Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline and a highly optimized FAISS vector index, Grotius actively bridges the gap between complex legal vernacular and natural human inquiry. Powered by a robust Python backend and currently analyzing over 1.06 million distinct legal precedents, Grotius doesn't just search the law—it understands it.
